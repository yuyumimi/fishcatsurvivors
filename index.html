About Fish Cat Survivors

Fish Cat Survivors is a unique online survival game where you control a hybrid creature—part fish, part cat—battling to survive in a vibrant underwater world. Outsmart your enemies, collect power-ups, and see how long you can last!

  • Challenging survival mechanics and fun power-ups
